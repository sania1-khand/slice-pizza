Our Story

Founded in 2010, Slice of Heaven was born from a passion for authentic Italian pizza. Our head chef, Marco Bianchi, trained in Naples and brought his family's century-old recipes to NYC. What started as a small neighborhood pizzeria has now become a beloved hotspot for pizza lovers across the city.

We use only the finest imported Italian ingredients and traditional wood-fired techniques. Every bite is a celebration of rich flavors, crispy yet chewy crusts, and fresh, high-quality toppings.
